The SnapBrim comes in two styles: full brim and cap style. The SnapBrim fits MSA and Bullard hart hats and caps. It is easily removable and almost indestructible. Both SnapBrim styles come with a detachable orange back of neck sunshade. Wide brim and neckshade help protect against the dangerous rays of the sun that is the leading cause of skin cancer. The sun shade is attached with Velcro. Each SnapBrim is tested to ASTM F2178 and is tested to 33 cal/cm2. No ignition from 27.7 and 44 cal/cm2.
